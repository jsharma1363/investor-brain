## Key Frameworks

### Duration Taxonomy (5 categories)
1. Months — trading, catalysts
2. Quarters — earnings plays
3. 1-3 years — re-ratings, turnarounds
4. 3-7 years — compounders, secular trends
5. Decades — generational holdings

### Three-Path Alpha Framework
In an AI-saturated world, increasingly three paths to alpha:
1. **Nose for where/when to dig** — knowing which situations warrant deep work before others
2. **Concentration** — only the very best ideas
3. **No nostalgia** — remove attachment to past performance, focus only on future expected returns, accept when market pulls forward returns

### My Take
- The three paths are sequential, not parallel: path 1 feeds path 2, path 3 is the discipline that lets 1 and 2 work
- "Duration isn't the path to anything other than index level returns" is partially wrong — duration IS an alpha source, but only as a byproduct of concentration + forward-return discipline
